Avni Mittal

I am a Computer Science researcher and Data Scientist (AI Research) at Microsoft Security, where I build multi-agent, LLM-based systems for security analysis and policy compliance. Broadly, my research focuses on multi-agent systems, AI alignment and safety, interpretability, and reasoning in large language models. I am especially interested in developing deployable, trustworthy AI systems whose internal reasoning processes are transparent, reliable under distribution shifts, and aligned with human values.
